# HaM5dsp
## Advanced Audio DSP Software for HF Reception

**Turn any receiver into a precision listening instrument.**

**HaM5dsp** is a portable DSP audio processor designed for serious radio operators who demand clarity, separation, and control—even from receivers without built-in DSP.  
Simply connect it to any line-out signal from a receiver and rediscover HF bands.

---

## ✅ Key Features

- Works with virtually any receiver with mono line-level (or lower) output  
- Portable, battery-powered design (with recommended module)  
- Reusable hardware platform  
- Instant improvement in intelligibility and operator comfort
- Very low latency (around 16 msec.), excellent for CW fast QSO exchange (~40 WPM → ~30 ms / dit)

---

## 🎧 Transform Your CW Experience

### Spatial Audio Processing – Hear Signals, Not Noise

In crowded bands and pile-ups, signals no longer blur together.

- **Stereo 2D Engine (Time-Based Separation)**  
  Creates a natural stereo soundfield that reduces listening fatigue and improves focus.

- **Stereo 3D Engine (Frequency-Based Spatialization)**  
  Signals close in frequency are spread across the stereo field, making them easier to distinguish.

➡️ Result: Each station becomes easier to track and copy.

- **Adjustable Spatial Depth**  
  Fine-tune separation intensity from subtle to aggressive.

## ⚙️ Hardware Platform

### Design Philosophy

Instead of building custom hardware from scratch, HaM5dsp uses a **proven industrial platform** to ensure:

- Stability  
- Reliability  
- Performance  

Based on the **M5Stack ecosystem** (independent choice, not affiliated).

---

### Core Components

- **ESP32 Dual-Core @ 240MHz** – real-time DSP processing  
- **ES8388 Audio CODEC** – high-quality audio conversion  
- **AXP192 PMIC** – battery and power management  
- **Integrated RTC** – time consistency  
- **Touchscreen Display** – intuitive control  
- **RGB LEDs (WS2812)** – visual system feedback  

---

### Modular Configuration

- **M5Stack Core2 (v1.3)** – main unit  
- **Audio Module** – audio interface  
- **Optional Base2 Module** – battery and charging  

➡️ Fully self-contained portable system.
